White Chocolate Sauce

A smooth classic with a tiny bourbon kick!

I learned this one from an old coworker of mine. He always had a sweet tooth and so any time I needed some inspiration for a dessert I'd ask him. This recipe is one of my favorites that he has shown me.

Ingredients

  • 12oz white chocolate chips
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 2oz of your favorite bourbon

Steps

  1. Add chocolate chips to a small saucepot.
  2. How much cream you add is entirely up to you. I like my sauce just a little bit thick so I dont add much cream. If you like your sauce thinner then it won't hurt to add extra cream here.
  3. Put on low heat and try not to simmer. You aren't cooking anything here. You are just melting the chocolate into the cream.
  4. Once the chocolate is fully melted, add your bourbon.
